0D EBM (simple)¶

A 0D EBM equiped with:

  • constant incoming solar radiation flux
  • outgoing radiation flux according to the Stefan-Boltzmann law

0D EBM (\(CO_2\) forced)¶

A 0D EBM equiped with:

  • constant incoming solar radiation flux
  • outgoing radiation flux according to the Stefan-Boltzmann law
  • \(CO_2\)-forcing according to radiative forcing estimates by Myhre

The tutorial-file of this EBM uses a \(CO_2\)-forcing based on 1958 - present atmospheric \(CO_2\)-concentrations (the Keeling-curve references:Keeling-curve>)
